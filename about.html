About Hon. Keynan Abdi Mohamed

Hon. Keynan Abdi Mohamed is a passionate Ugandan leader, community organizer, and public servant dedicated to the progress and unity of Uganda. With a background rooted in advocacy for youth empowerment and education, he has risen to serve his community in various leadership roles.

  • Advocate for Quality Education
  • Champion for Youth Empowerment
  • Promoter of Unity & Inclusion
  • Defender of Good Governance

Born and raised in Uganda, Keynan understands the needs and aspirations of ordinary Ugandans. He stands for transparency, equal opportunity, and inclusive development for all regions of the country.

As a candidate for the 2026 elections, Hon. Keynan Abdi Mohamed leads a diverse and energetic team focused on unity, service delivery, and restoring trust in government institutions. His vision is to build a prosperous, peaceful, and inclusive Uganda for all.
