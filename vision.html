Our Vision for Uganda

Together, we can build a Uganda where every citizen has the opportunity to thrive. My vision is rooted in unity, progress, and empowerment for all. Join me as we shape a brighter, more inclusive future for our nation.

Quality Education

Free and quality education for all children, better teacher support, and investment in skills and vocational training.

Accessible Healthcare

Modern health facilities, affordable care, and improved access for rural communities to ensure a healthy nation.

Youth Empowerment

Job creation, entrepreneurship programs, and leadership opportunities for young Ugandans to drive our future.

Infrastructure

Upgrading roads, water systems, and housing to connect communities and support economic growth.

Good Governance

Transparency, anti-corruption reforms, and decentralization to bring services closer to the people.
